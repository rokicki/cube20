CubeLovers was a mailing list dedicated to the mathematics of Rubik's Cube and similar puzzles active from 1980 to 1999. The mailing list lives on in forum form here. In order to preserve the original fascinating material, I am making a copy of the archive here for people to peruse.

The dates, authors, and titles of each posting are available in one big list here, with each line clicking through to the message itself.
